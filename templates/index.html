About ArtBzaar

ArtBzaar is a place where creativity comes alive. It's a space for artists to share their work, tell their stories, and connect with people who truly appreciate art. Whether you paint, sketch, click photos, or create digital art, ArtBzaar gives you a stage to show what you love and inspire others. For art lovers, it's a spot to discover unique pieces, explore new ideas, and support talented creators. At its heart, ArtBzaar is all about community, inspiration, and the joy of creating—a place where everyone can celebrate art, one piece at a time.

About the Developer

ArtBzaar is developed by Rachit Singh Rana, a Computer Science undergraduate at NIT Delhi. With a passion for frontend development, fine arts, and digital design, he created this platform to bridge the gap between creativity and technology. Rachit has led multiple creative initiatives — Graphic Head of the Cultural Club — and continues to explore the intersection of art and innovation.
